Clinical Setting & Introduction

🌡️ Cancer Vaccines: HPV Example ▼
中文

HPV疫苗是一個重要的臨床應用實例。它展示了DNA技術如何用於開發預防性疫苗。HPV（人乳頭瘤病毒）與多種癌症有關，特別是子宮頸癌。

DNA技術使科學家能夠製造針對特定病毒蛋白的疫苗，預防感染。

HPV vaccine is a key clinical application example. It demonstrates how DNA technology is used to develop preventive vaccines. HPV (human papillomavirus) is associated with various cancers, particularly cervical cancer.

DNA technology enables scientists to manufacture vaccines targeting specific viral proteins to prevent infection.

🔬 From E. coli to Gene Mapping ▼
中文

E. coli研究的發現：

  • 研究發現大腸桿菌具有性機制，可以實現基因轉移
  • 這種轉移將兩個不同細胞的基因結合在一起
  • 這個發現導致了重組DNA技術的開發

重組DNA技術是從不同來源組合基因的一套技術集合。

English

Key findings from E. coli research:

  • Research revealed that these bacteria have a sexual mechanism for gene transfer
  • This transfer combines genes from two different cells
  • This discovery led to the development of recombinant DNA technology

Recombinant DNA technology is a set of techniques for combining genes from different sources.

Bacteria as Tools for DNA Manipulation

🦠 Three Ways of DNA Transfer in Bacteria ▼

在自然界中，細菌可以通過三種方式轉移DNA | Three ways bacteria transfer DNA

中文

1. 轉化 (Transformation) - 細菌吸收環境中來自其他細菌細胞的DNA片段

2. 轉導 (Transduction) - 噬菌體（病毒）轉移來自另一個細菌細胞的基因片段

3. 接合 (Conjugation) - 細胞的聯合和它們之間的DNA轉移，涉及性菌毛形成橋梁

English

1. Transformation - Taking up of DNA from the fluid surrounding the cell

2. Transduction - Transfer of genes by a phage (virus)

3. Conjugation - Union of cells and DNA transfer via mating bridge formed by sex pili

🔄 Plasmids as Gene Carriers ▼
中文

質粒定義： 與細菌染色體分離的小型環形DNA分子

用途：

  • 質粒是DNA技術的關鍵工具
  • 研究人員使用質粒將基因插入細菌

English

Plasmid Definition: A small circular DNA molecule separate from the bacterial chromosome

Applications:

  • Plasmids are key tools for DNA technology
  • Researchers use plasmids to insert genes into bacteria
  • Bacteria reproduce and copy the genes they carry

Molecular Techniques

✂️ Restriction Enzymes & DNA Ligase ▼
中文

限制酶：

  • 在特定位點切割DNA
  • 產生"粘性末端"（sticky ends）

DNA連接酶：

  • 將DNA片段粘合在一起
  • 共價結合DNA鏈
English

Restriction Enzymes:

  • Cut DNA at specific recognition sequences
  • Produce "sticky ends" that allow DNA fragments to bind

DNA Ligase:

  • Pastes DNA fragments together
  • Forms covalent bonds between DNA strands
🔬 PCR - Polymerase Chain Reaction ▼
中文

PCR方法： 聚合酶鏈反應可以快速複製測試管中的小DNA樣本。

三個關鍵步驟（每個循環）：

  • 變性（95°C）：DNA鏈分離
  • 退火（55°C）：引物與模板結合
  • 延伸（72°C）：Taq聚合酶合成新DNA鏈
English

PCR Method: Polymerase chain reaction can quickly clone a small DNA sample in a test tube.

Three Key Steps (per cycle):

  • Denaturing (95°C): DNA strands separate
  • Annealing (55°C): Primers bind to template
  • Extension (72°C): Taq polymerase synthesizes new DNA strand
🧲 Gel Electrophoresis ▼
中文

凝膠電泳：

  • 根據大小對DNA分子進行分類
  • 帶負電的DNA在電場中移動
  • 較短的分子移動更快
English

Gel Electrophoresis:

  • Sorts DNA molecules by size
  • Negatively charged DNA moves through gel in electric field
  • Shorter molecules move faster

Advanced Techniques: CRISPR & CAR-T

✂️ CRISPR System: Gene Editing Revolution ▼
中文

CRISPR是什麼： 一個精確的基因編輯工具，允許科學家在特定位置切割和修改DNA。

工作原理： 引導RNA指向特定DNA序列，Cas9在目標位置產生雙鏈斷裂，細胞通過NHEJ或HDR進行修復。

English

What is CRISPR: A precise gene-editing tool allowing scientists to cut and modify DNA at specific locations.

How it works: Guide RNA directs Cas9 to target sequence, creates double-strand break, cell repairs via NHEJ or HDR.

💊 CAR-T Cell Therapy ▼
中文

CAR-T療法定義： 癌症免疫療法，患者的T細胞在遺傳上改造以更好地識別和攻擊癌細胞。

步驟： 從患者血液取出T細胞 → 遺傳修改 → 擴展數量 → 輸回患者

English

CAR-T Definition: Cancer immunotherapy where patient's T-cells are genetically modified to recognize and attack cancer cells.

Steps: Remove T cells → Genetically modify → Expand → Infuse back into patient
