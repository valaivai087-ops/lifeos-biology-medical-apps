📖 How to Read This Mind Map

Understanding the Structure

This mind map shows the complete overview of gene expression control organized into five major branches:

  • Prokaryotic Systems: Bacteria use operons for coordinated gene control with negative (trp) and positive (lac) regulation
  • Eukaryotic Systems: Multi-level control from chromatin modifications through transcription to post-transcriptional processing
  • Non-coding RNAs: miRNA, piRNA, and lncRNA regulate genes at various stages
  • Development: Cytoplasmic determinants and master genes direct cell differentiation
  • Cancer: Dysregulation of proto-oncogenes and tumor suppressors in multistep model

🎓 Three-Level Framework

Level 1: Chromatin Control (最前面的控制)

Before genes can be transcribed, the chromatin structure must be accessible. Histone acetylation opens chromatin (ON), while methylation closes it (OFF). This is the first gatekeeper.

Level 2: Transcription Control (中間的控制)

General transcription factors bind to TATA box. Specific transcription factors bind to enhancers and activate RNA polymerase II. This controls WHEN and HOW MUCH transcription occurs.

Level 3: Post-Transcriptional (最後面的控制)

RNA processing through alternative splicing creates protein diversity. mRNA stability is controlled by 3' UTR sequences and miRNAs. Protein degradation via ubiquitin-proteasome controls protein levels.

🎓 三層級框架

第1級：核小體控制 (最前面的控制)

在基因轉錄前，核小體結構必須可接近。組蛋白乙醯化打開核小體(ON)，甲基化關閉它(OFF)。這是第一道守門員。

第2級：轉錄控制 (中間的控制)

通用轉錄因子結合TATA盒。特異性轉錄因子結合增強子並激活RNA聚合酶II。這控制何時和多少轉錄發生。

第3級：轉錄後 (最後面的控制)

通過選擇性剪接的RNA加工創造蛋白質多樣性。mRNA穩定性由3' UTR序列和miRNA控制。通過泛素-蛋白酶體的蛋白質降解控制蛋白質水平。

🧬 Operon Components & Mechanism

Three Essential Parts

1. Promoter (啟動子): Where RNA polymerase begins transcription

2. Operator (操作子): The on-off switch where repressor protein binds

3. Structural Genes (結構基因): Genes that produce the enzymes

How It Works

A repressor protein is made from a regulatory gene. When the corepressor (like tryptophan) binds to this repressor, the repressor becomes active and blocks the operator. This prevents RNA polymerase from transcribing the genes.

🧬 操作子組件與機制

三個必要部分

1. 啟動子：RNA聚合酶開始轉錄的地方

2. 操作子：抑制子蛋白結合的開關

3. 結構基因：產生酵素的基因

工作原理

抑制子蛋白由調控基因製造。當輔助抑制子(如色氨酸)結合到抑制子時，抑制子變活躍並阻止操作子。這防止RNA聚合酶轉錄基因。

🧫 Eukaryotic Regulation - 真核生物調控

📚 Six Levels of Eukaryotic Gene Regulation

1. Chromatin Modification 核小體修飾

Histone acetylation opens chromatin structure, allowing access for transcription factors. Histone methylation typically closes chromatin and silences genes. DNA methylation adds methyl groups to cytosine bases, usually repressing transcription.

2. Transcription Initiation 轉錄起始

General transcription factors recognize and bind the TATA box in the promoter. Specific transcription factors bind to enhancer sequences, which can be far from the promoter. DNA looping brings enhancers near the promoter through protein interactions.

3. RNA Processing RNA加工

Alternative RNA splicing allows one gene to produce multiple proteins by including or excluding different exons. The primary transcript is processed with 5' cap and 3' poly-A tail addition.

4. mRNA Stability mRNA穩定性

Sequences in the 3' UTR (untranslated region) affect how long the mRNA survives in the cytoplasm. microRNAs bind to mRNA and either cause degradation or block translation initiation.

5. Translation Control 翻譯控制

Regulatory proteins can block the initiation of translation by binding to specific sequences on the mRNA. The life span of mRNA molecules determines protein synthesis rates in cells.

6. Protein Degradation 蛋白質降解

Ubiquitin is attached to proteins to mark them for degradation by proteasomes. This selective degradation regulates protein levels in the cell.

📚 真核生物基因調控六個層級

1. 核小體修飾

組蛋白乙醯化打開核小體結構，允許轉錄因子進入。組蛋白甲基化通常關閉核小體並沈默基因。DNA甲基化在胞嘧啶鹼基上添加甲基，通常抑制轉錄。

2. 轉錄起始

通用轉錄因子識別並結合啟動子中的TATA盒。特異性轉錄因子結合增強子序列，可能距離啟動子很遠。DNA環帶使增強子靠近啟動子。

3. RNA加工

選擇性RNA剪接允許一個基因產生多種蛋白質，通過包括或排除不同外顯子。主轉錄本被加工並添加5'帽和3'多聚A尾。

4. mRNA穩定性

3' UTR中的序列影響mRNA在細胞質中存活多長時間。微RNA與mRNA結合，導致降解或阻止翻譯起始。

5. 翻譯控制

調控蛋白可以通過結合mRNA上的特定序列來阻止翻譯起始。mRNA分子的壽命決定了細胞中的蛋白質合成速率。

6. 蛋白質降解

泛素附著到蛋白質上以標記它們被蛋白酶體降解。這種選擇性降解調節細胞中的蛋白質水平。

🔀 Key Comparisons - 關鍵比較

🔀 trp vs lac Operon

TRP OPERON

Pathway: Anabolic (synthesis of amino acid)

Default State: ON (actively making tryptophan)

When to Turn OFF: When tryptophan is abundant (corepressor present)

Logic: "We have enough, STOP making"

LAC OPERON

Pathway: Catabolic (breakdown of lactose)

Default State: OFF (not making digestive enzymes)

When to Turn ON: When lactose arrives (inducer present)

Logic: "Food arrived, START breaking it down"

🔀 trp與lac操作子

TRP操作子

途徑：合成代謝(合成氨基酸)

默認狀態：ON(積極製造色氨酸)

何時關閉：當色氨酸豐富時(輔助抑制子存在)

邏輯："我們有足夠的，停止製造"

LAC操作子

途徑：分解代謝(分解乳糖)

默認狀態：OFF(不製造消化酵素)

何時打開：當乳糖到達時(誘導子存在)

邏輯："食物到了，開始分解它"

💡 Easy Memory Tricks - 簡單記憶技巧

TRP = Tryptophan (氨基酸) | Made (製造) Think: Storage tank that FILLS UP with tryptophan
→ When FULL (high tryptophan) → STOP making (turn OFF)
LAC = Lactose (食物) | Eat (吃) Think: Factory that gets DELIVERY of lactose
→ When DELIVERY arrives → START breaking down (turn ON)
REPRESSIBLE = REPRESS When FULL Repress = suppress | Full = abundance | Like turning down heat when room is warm enough
INDUCIBLE = INDUCE When NEEDED Induce = turn on | Needed = substrate present | Like turning on heat when room is cold
ACTIVATOR = A (Activation) | Turns ON A = Activation | Adds energy to system | Like stepping on gas pedal
REPRESSOR = R (Repression) | Turns OFF R = Repression | Takes energy away | Like stepping on brake pedal

🎯 Key Takeaways - 關鍵要點

1. Bacteria control genes quickly with operons - Coordinated control of multiple genes by one switch allows rapid response to environmental changes.

2. Eukaryotes control genes at multiple levels - From chromatin structure to protein degradation, providing sophisticated and flexible control.

3. Non-coding RNAs are major regulators - miRNA alone affects ~50% of human genes, showing regulation extends beyond coding genes.

4. Cell differentiation relies on differential gene expression - Same genome, different genes expressed, creates different cell types.

5. Cancer results from multiple genetic hits - Not one mutation, but accumulation of mutations in both oncogenes and tumor suppressors.

1. 細菌用操作子快速控制基因 - 由一個開關協調多個基因的控制允許快速響應環境變化。

2. 真核生物在多個級別控制基因 - 從核小體結構到蛋白質降解，提供精細且靈活的控制。

3. 非編碼RNA是主要調控器 - miRNA單獨影響約50%的人類基因，表明調控超越編碼基因。

4. 細胞分化依賴於差異性基因表現 - 相同的基因組，表現不同的基因，創造不同的細胞類型。

5. 癌症是多個遺傳命中的結果 - 不是一個突變，而是癌基因和腫瘤抑制基因中多個突變的累積。
